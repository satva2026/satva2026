Indian Road Survey & Management (IRSM) Pvt Ltd

We are an industry leader providing services related to road condition and assessment since 2009. More than 100+ qualified and trained engineers from various related fields constitute a formidable work force. NTRO (formerly ARRB), Australia are our technical partners. We enjoy the benefit of having access to the 65 years of Research & Technical Expertise of NTRO.

We are both ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 39001:2012 certified. We were one of the first companies in the transportation space to acquire ISO 39001 certification in India. We bring together a pool of qualified professionals with deep expertise in safety and iRAP standards. These credentials make us one of the most sought-after service providers in the road infrastructure space and for iRAP projects.

IRSM boasts a full-fledged fleet of advanced technology equipment, including multiple sets of NSV, HWD, Axle Load systems, and more. These state-of-the-art resources are readily available for comprehensive road condition assessments, catering to both existing and prospective clients, both at Project level and Network level.

IRSM has a proven track record of having successfully completed prestigious projects covering over 600,000 km of road network condition assessments, including Road Safety and iRAP evaluations. Additionally, IRSM has conducted comprehensive Airport Pavement Evaluations for more than 15 airports, spanning international, domestic, and greenfield facilities. Beyond assessments, IRSM offers expertise in Pavement Management Systems and provides specialized training in road safety, condition assessment, standardization, and data-driven decision-making.
