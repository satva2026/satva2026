Hostel IIT Palakkad

Note: Participants requiring on-campus accommodation in the hostel may select the "Accommodation Required" option in the registration form. Rooms are allocated on a first-come, first-serve basis, on a chargeable basis.

Our fully residential campus offers dedicated hostels for both girls and boys, creating a secure, comfortable, vibrant, and exceptional living experience. In total, the campus houses six hostels: four in the tranquil Nila Campus and two in the serene Sahyadri Campus. Students are provided with double/triple sharing accommodation in Nila Campus, while rooms at Sahyadri Campus are either single or triple occupancy rooms.

Our hostels are equipped with well-appointed rooms, water heaters, RO-based drinking water systems, heavy-duty washing machines, and both WiFi and LAN network connections, ensuring convenience and connectivity. Additionally, a spacious dining hall, a well-equipped recreational area, an indoor games area, and a fitness center are readily available for leisure and physical activities. Each room is equipped with either WiFi or LAN connectivity, offering seamless online access. Additionally, common rooms in every hostel provide a space for relaxation, equipped with a television, newspapers, and a selection of magazines chosen by the student body.

To promote students' overall well-being, amenities such as an in-house facility for football, volleyball, basketball, and table tennis are provided. Professional coaches are on hand to guide students in athletics, football, volleyball, and cricket. Badminton enthusiasts can pursue their interests at the 4GB Badminton Academy in Palakkad. To accommodate visitors, guest rooms are available for Institute faculty, staff, and students' families, ensuring a warm welcome.

Guest House - Hamsanandi

The Guest House at IIT Palakkad, located just 1 km away from the campus with the scenic Western Ghats as its backdrop, offers a tranquil retreat for visitors. These guest houses, featuring a contemporary aesthetic, offer top-of-the-line amenities and ensure a comfortable stay for visitors. A total of 16 rooms are distributed across four blocks – Block A, B, C, and D – providing ample accommodation.

The Guest House caters to the accommodation needs of the institute's guests, employees' guests, alumni, and, upon request, parents of students. Each unit is equipped with air-conditioned bedrooms, an attached toilet with a geyser, and a hall and kitchen. Moreover, all rooms are serviced by an emergency generator set, ensuring uninterrupted comfort.

The Guest House also offers additional shared facilities for guests' convenience. These include a meeting room that can accommodate 50 people for gatherings or conferences, and a separate room equipped for gymnastics and other physical exercises. The property is managed by the Guest House Manager and their dedicated team. The allotment of guest house units is subject to room availability.
