IIT Palakkad

Established in 2015, the Indian Institute of Technology, Palakkad is dedicated to creating an environment that enables students and faculty to engage in the pursuit of knowledge, to dream, think, and innovate thereby becoming change agents for a better world.

The Institute recognises collective growth, in collaboration with industry and other academic institutions, as the need of the time and emphasizes blue-sky research and directed research as two essential pillars of technology development.

Located in the historic and vibrant region of Palakkad, known as the granary of Kerala, our institute is perfectly situated as the gateway to the state from the North, Palakkad Gap in the Western Ghats. Surrounded by rich traditions, significant historical events, and its sylvan surroundings, especially the Silent Valley rain forests and ubiquitous palmyra trees, Palakkad provides an inspiring backdrop for academic pursuits.

Website: https://iitpkd.ac.in/

The Institution of Engineers (India)

The Institution of Engineers (India) [IEI], established in 1920 and headquartered in Kolkata, is the country’s largest multidisciplinary professional body, representing 15 engineering disciplines and over 2.6 lakh members. Incorporated by Royal Charter in 1935, it remains the only professional body in India with this distinction. IEI provides wide-ranging technical and professional services to government, industry, academia, and engineers through 124 Centres in India and 6 overseas chapters. It maintains bilateral agreements with 31 international bodies and is recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) by the Government of India, offering Grant-in-Aid support to UG, PG, and PhD students. IEI holds the Indian Register for International Professional Engineers (IntPE) and awards Professional Engineer (PE) certification. In collaboration with Springer, it publishes peer-reviewed international journals across five series covering fifteen engineering disciplines.

IEI Palakkad Local Centre

Established as a Paper Centre in 1981 and upgraded in 1988, the Palakkad Local Centre serves the Palakkad and Malappuram districts. With around 1,500 corporate members across 14 Engineering Divisions and 9 Institutional Members—including IIT Palakkad—the Centre continues to expand. Located at Nerukakkad, Kalleppully, it houses a well-equipped library for members and students, and regularly organizes technical meetings, national seminars, workshops, and statutory day observances as guided by IEI Headquarters.

Website: https://www.ieindia.org/

IGS Palakkad Chapter

The IGS Palakkad Chapter is a local chapter of the Indian Geotechnical Society (IGS), established in 2024. The chapter was officially inaugurated on April 10th, 2024, at the Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad. The IGS Palakkad Chapter aims to create a vibrant community of professionals and enthusiasts in the field of geotechnical engineering by organizing events, workshops, and conferences that facilitate the exchange of ideas and the latest advancements in the field. It also seeks to provide a forum for addressing local challenges in geotechnical engineering while promoting innovation and best practices. Through its activities, the chapter strives to contribute to the development of the geotechnical profession and support the continued growth of expertise in geotechnical engineering. By encouraging networking and collaboration, the IGS Palakkad Chapter hopes to strengthen the ties between academia, industry, and government, creating a sustainable and dynamic environment for future progress in geotechnical engineering.

For more details: https://www.igs.org.in/local-chapter/palakkad
