About Symposium

The fourth edition of SATVA 2026, organized by the Environmental Sciences and Sustainable Engineering Centre (ESSENCE) at IIT Palakkad, is set to redefine sustainable engineering.

In collaboration with IEI Palakkad and IGS Palakkad, this symposium creates a vibrant platform for technical exchange. We bring together experts to discuss innovations in road infrastructure, focusing on resilient, low-carbon solutions.

Download Flyer

Pavement Sustainability Frontiers: From Materials to Maintenance

The symposium aims to provide a platform for researchers, engineers, and industry professionals to exchange ideas and share advances in sustainable pavement technologies. Emphasis will be placed on developing resilient, low-carbon, and resource-efficient pavement systems through innovations in materials, design, construction, and maintenance practices.
